About

Artemis Morrow is a newly-minted web developer with a strong aesthetic sense and creative background. She received a BA in Theatre from SUNY College at Plattsburgh with a particular focus on costume design and construction in May of 2006.

After almost two decades of experimenting with basic HTML, she decided to channel her enjoyment of problem-solving and making things into learning to code in 2020 and received a certificate in full-stack web development from the University of Pennsylvania's coding bootcamp in May of 2021. She looks forward to bringing her wide-ranging and varied work experience into the exciting, growing world of programming.
